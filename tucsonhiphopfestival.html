The Tucson Hip Hop Festival is an annual event representing and holding space for the four elements of hip hop: graffiti, deejaying, breaking, and rapping. The first festival was held in 2015, brought in 600+ attendees, and showcased 50 local artists. Every year the festival grows and always centers its experience around being community-driven, supporting local businesses, providing resources and panels, and showcasing artists locally, nation-wide, and across the border. To stay up to date with all their news and events visit their website, Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter!
